Come Climb With Us!

The Alpine Club at CU’s mission is to bring together Boulder climbers of all experience and abilities-from Gym Rats to Big Wallers-to form a supportive community of outdoor athletes.

Through the organization of group trips, clinics, speakers and community outreach events, the Alpine Club strives to encourage individuals to take their climbing to the next level. The focus of our trips is to provide club members with an inexpensive way to explore climbing areas outside of Boulder. Nearly every weekend throughout the semester we organize trips to world-class climbing destinations, such as Indian Creek, the Devil’s Tower, the Black Canyon and Ouray. We also frequent many of the great climbing areas close to Boulder, providing opportunities for those who are new to the sport to increase their climbing experience, make friends and meet partners.
